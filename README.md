# Net-Revenue-Management-NRM-Case-Study-FMCG-Commercial-Strategy-Model

Executive Summary

This project analyzes revenue growth and margin optimization opportunities within an FMCG shampoo category using Net Revenue Management (NRM) principles. The objective was to identify structural profitability levers across pricing, mix, trade investment, and promotional strategy.
Through multi-dimensional KPI analysis (market share, YTD growth, MAT, gross margin, portfolio mix), the model quantifies where revenue is being diluted and where margin expansion opportunities exist.
The analysis demonstrates how structured revenue diagnostics can uncover growth buckets that materially improve profitability without requiring volume expansion.

Overview
This project simulates the role of a Category Manager at Healthmax, an FMCG company operating in the shampoo market. The objective was to conduct a full commercial performance analysis and build a Net Revenue Management (NRM) strategy to drive profitable growth.
The analysis moves beyond reporting KPIs and into structured commercial decision-making across pricing, portfolio optimisation, trade investments, and revenue forecasting.
